About LockedIN

We're building an interactive and connected exam space where students and organizers can seamlessly collaborate. Our goal is to simplify exam creation, student management, and real-time monitoring, ensuring a smooth and transparent examination process. We're working on features like customizable exam settings, live proctoring, and automated reports to help institutions streamline assessments while maintaining fairness and security. Whether you're managing a small class or a large-scale exam, our platform will adapt to your needs.
